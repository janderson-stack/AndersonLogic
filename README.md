# ANDERSONLOGICai
### Logic. Applied.

**AI consulting for education — and strategic social media design for organizations ready to grow.**

ANDERSONLOGICai is the brand umbrella for the consulting and design work of **Jeff Anderson**, a 24-year classroom educator and AP World History teacher. Every engagement starts the same way: **strategy before software.**

---

## What lives under this brand

### 🎓 AI Consulting (education-first)
Helping schools, districts, and educators adopt AI the right way — pedagogy before tools, guardrails before scale, and teacher voice amplified rather than replaced.
- Professional development for teachers
- Classroom AI workflow design (students coached, never graded, by AI)
- District AI strategy & academic-integrity audits
- AI strategy for businesses and organizations

### 🎨 Social Media Design
Brand-consistent social systems — templates, content calendars, and campaign creative — for businesses, schools, and programs. Across Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, and LinkedIn.

### 🏛️ BeHistorical *(flagship project)*
A from-scratch, interactive AP World History: Modern lesson engine with Socratic AI coaching, Canvas LMS integration, and school-endorsed guardrails. The working proof behind the consulting practice.
→ **Live:** https://janderson-stack.github.io/ap-world-history/
